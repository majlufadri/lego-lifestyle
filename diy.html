Build 1: Robo Car (LEGO Classic 1260)

Build a futuristic Robo Car with rolling wheels and a sleek design, perfect for LEGO Classic fans.

  1. Start with a 4x8 flat plate as the base.
  2. Attach 4 wheel pieces to the underside for mobility.
  3. Build the body using 2x4 gray bricks for a robotic look.
  4. Add a 2x2 clear piece as a cockpit window.
  5. Decorate with small red and blue bricks for details.

Build 2: Brick Building (LEGO Classic 10696)

Create a colorful Brick Building, ideal for beginners to showcase their creativity.

  1. Use a 6x6 green plate as the foundation.
  2. Stack 2x4 yellow bricks to form the walls.
  3. Add 2x2 red bricks for window frames.
  4. Top with a 4x4 blue plate as the roof.
  5. Decorate with small white bricks for accents.
